Building in Public: Starting Quanta From Zero

I’m building Quanta, a clinical laboratory ERP, and I’m going to document the entire journey publicly. This isn’t because I think my specific project is uniquely interesting—though I hope it will be useful—but because building in public has become one of the most powerful ways to create something people actually want while building genuine connections with the community you’re trying to serve. Why Build in Public? The traditional approach to building software, especially for specialized markets like clinical laboratories, is to work in stealth mode for months or years, perfect your product in isolation, then launch with a big reveal.
