Quanta: Clinical Laboratory ERP#

Status: Early development
Tech Stack: Python, Frappe Framework, MariaDB, FastAPI

Quanta is a specialized ERP system designed specifically for clinical laboratories. Unlike generic laboratory information systems, Quanta is built by someone who has actually worked in labs and understands the unique operational challenges they face.

Key Features#

  • Reagent and Inventory Management: Real-time tracking with automated reorder points and expiration monitoring
  • Quality Control Tracking: Comprehensive QC management with automated compliance reporting
  • ML-powered AMR Surveillance: Premium feature providing antimicrobial resistance monitoring and trend analysis
  • Instrument Integration: Seamless data flow from laboratory analyzers to reporting systems
  • Workflow Optimization: Laboratory operations designed by lab professionals for lab professionals

Business Model#

Quanta follows an open source core with SaaS premium features approach:

  • Core laboratory operations modules are open source
  • Advanced features like ML-powered AMR surveillance available via subscription
  • Custom integrations and enterprise support available
